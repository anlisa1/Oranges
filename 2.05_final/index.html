Origins of Oranges

Oranges are believed to have originated from Asia, especially the Malay Archipelago. The expansion of Islam, roman conquests and the development of Arab Trade routes contributed significantly to how far the oranges spread across the continents

There are over 400 different varieties of oranges such as:

  • Cara Cara Navel
  • Tangerines
  • Mandarin Orange
  • Clementine Oranges
